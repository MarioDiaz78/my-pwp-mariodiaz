Milestone-1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose: Develop a Professional Portfolio Website.

Audience: The focus will be towards targeting Web Development or Software Developing companies like hiring managers or recruiters.

Goal: The goal of this website is to provide employers a portfolio that will display attributes of mine that will benefit a future employer.

Persona

Name: Sam Farley

Age: 48

Profession: A hiring Manager at TechSmart Company who is looking to hire a full-time, 100% remote position, Website Developer. TechSmart is a full-service marketing and advertising agency.

Technology: Sam uses a Surface Studio 2 12th Gen Intel® Core™ at work. At home he has access to a Surface laptop Studio and has a Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra phone.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Sam is looking for the perfect fit for TechSmart. He wants an individual that is willing to learn new programs or programming languages as needed. The objective is to find someone who has a great attitude, works well in a team, and has excellent communication skills.

Frustrations and Needs: Sam needs a person that has a mastery of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Their proficiency with at least one front-end web framework such as React, Svelte, or equivalent. Most important is that he wants someone who is self-motivated with a great attitude and works well with others. The frustrating part is that individuals like that are hard to find.

Goals: Sam's goals as a hiring manager are to find the perfect candidate for the remote Website Developer position at TechSmart Company. He is looking for a skilled developer who is motivated, works well in a team, and has excellent communication skills. Sam wants someone who is adaptable and willing to learn new programs or programming languages as needed. He also wants the candidate to have a mastery of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and proficiency with at least one front-end web framework such as React, Svelte, or equivalent.

User Story: As a hiring manager at TechSmart Company, I wants to find the perfect fit for the remote Website Developer position.
